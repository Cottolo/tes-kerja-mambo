OUR ROLE

Mambo's objective is to promote cultural and
environmental heritage through community trade and
blockchain technology that preserves and promotes
heritage sites, green spaces, and manufacturing of
sustainable cultural products and experiences, for the
community, by the community that can be applied to any
location in the world that is looking for economic growth
as well as historical and cultural preservation.

The Problem: COVID-19 risk is highest in low-income
communities with limited sanitation – and social distancing will
not work. There are vulnerable communities across Kenya, as
well as “hot spot” risk areas that could cause a rapid rise in
COVID-19 infections. The solution: Rapid mass sanitation and
personal protection via a three-pronged approach.

Our hashtag#roadtrip4acause was curated around learning,
interaction, and travel. The 4-day journey of engagement
workshops drew great insights that begin to not only address
the urgent need around the climate justice, but also the inclusive
nature of how cultural innovation and creativity can bridge the
equity narrative to develop indigenous prosperity models.

Working alongside our client, Book bunk, we are working to
restore some of Nairobi’s most iconic public libraries into sites of
heritage, public art, collective memory, knowledge production,
shared experiences, cultural leadership and information
exchange. The McMillan Library Retrofit & Restoration included
both Kaloleni Social Hall as well as Makadara Library.
